Design

Mechanical Design is one of the fundamental skills in Engineering that enables the total definition of a product during its development. We are specialized in providing innovative solutions, combining the latest technology with the classic engineering methods. Our experienced team in co-operation with our clients are able to develop products from conceptual stage to production or to improve existing ones. All stages of design and development are carried out according to specifications and standards of our customer's application.

With rising costs and margin pressure across industries, manufacturing companies look beyond their shores to optimize their Product Development activities to shorten the time to market and get an early edge in the competitive business ecosystem.

Uniscient Engineering offers Product design from concept Design to final detailing which comprises of the following services :

  • Convective/Conductive/Radiative Heat Transfer
  • Steady-state and transient thermal models
  • Coupled radiation heat transfer
  • Single phase and multiphase flow heat transfer
  • Heat exchanger optimization
  • Automated parametric analysis

Our engineers collaborate with our customers and provide cost effective thermal solutions. Our recommendations help our clients to select appropriate materials, perform design modifications and thereby utilize our solutions for design optimization and getting finest results.
