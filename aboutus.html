about us

Uniscient Engineering is an integrated Engineering and Training solutions provider. We offer Services and Solutions that are simultaneously affordable, sustainable and of high quality with cost-conscious and eco-aware product development.

Our service offerings include new product development, product improvement, reverse engineering, cost reduction, legacy conversions and engineering process support using cutting-edge CAD / CAM / CAE technology in various domains. Our explicit expertise lies within the automotive, commercial vehicles, farm equipment, aerospace, construction & industrial equipment.

Team

Uniscient is promoted and managed by a team of proven talented technologists with a long-term vision, domain expertise and commitment to the core. With a dedicated team of professionals with vast technical experience, cross-functional capabilities and state-of-art-infrastructure gives UNISCIENT an edge in understanding the client’s requirement and demands in a dynamically changing engineering world.

What We Do

We are leading CAD/CAE and CFD experts serving a variety of industries like automotive, aerospace, electronics, consumer products and other allied sectors. Our strength enables us to help industries conceptualize and optimize their mechanical engineering design and engineering processes thereby making them more innovative, efficient and cost effective. Also, with an academic and corporate Training unit, we provide the best-in-class industry training to young graduates and industry professional as a part of skill up gradation.
