Electronic cooling

The rapid development of electronics and exponential increase of power densities is posing a huge challenge for the product designer to develop a robust thermal management of electronics. Proper thermal management improves the life and performance of the product by removing the heat generated by the devices.

Heat management of electronics is one of the key services we provide for all types of electronic equipments and components. Our comprehensive understanding of thermal flows enables us to detect issues at an early stage and hence offer cost effective solutions. We use fully interactive software tool and predict airflow and heat transfer at the component level, board level or cabinet level thereby improving the design performance and reducing the need for physical prototyping

Our team provides an in-depth assessment of the enclosures and all components of electronic systems using state of art CFD technology.

Services We Offer:
  • Thermal Distribution of electronic components
  • Hot Spot Identification
  • Air Flow Analysis
  • Design for compact enclosures
  • Optimized performance
  • Board level or Layout analysis
  • Compact and detailed PCB analysis
  • Improvised Heatsinks And Cooling System
  • LED Thermal Management
  • Evaluating Failure Risks
  • Component, Board And System Levels
  • Conduction Cooling
